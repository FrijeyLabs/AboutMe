My Journey in Tech

Turning ideas into reality through code

Building Solutions That Matter

I started this journey because of my love for video games and the desire to create them. While game development captured my heart in 2018-2019, I discovered a deeper passion for software development as a profession, and I've been building solutions ever since.

Off-screen, I'm — years old, happily married to my amazing wife Marlyn, and the proud father of our beautiful daughter Miranda. God and family is the engine behind everything I build.

I graduated in 2020 with a degree in Software Engineering from UAPA (Universidad Abierta Para Adultos) in the Dominican Republic, where I built the strong academic foundation that powers my work every day.

My expertise spans across web development, mobile applications, and desktop software. I thrive on challenging and unconventional projects—I'm not interested in typical store apps. I seek complex problems that push my skills forward and deliver real value.

From powerful ASP.NET web applications to modern React and Next.js stacks running on Node.js, and from sleek Windows Forms desktop apps to responsive mobile experiences, I bring ideas to life across all platforms. As an AI-forward developer, I've adopted Claude Code as a core part of my modern workflow, pairing with AI to write smarter, faster, and more reliable software.
